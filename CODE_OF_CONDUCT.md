# Code of Conduct

LumaTrace is committed to maintaining a professional, respectful, and collaborative environment for enterprise clients, partners, and contributors.

---

## Expected Behavior

Participants in issues, discussions, and pull requests are expected to:

- Communicate professionally and respectfully
- Provide constructive technical feedback
- Avoid inflammatory, discriminatory, or hostile language

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language
- Intentional disruption of discussions
- Disclosure of confidential or proprietary information

---

## Enforcement

Repository maintainers reserve the right to moderate, edit, or remove content that violates this Code of Conduct.

Repeated violations may result in restricted access to repository interactions.
